The Interactive Media program begins with the premise that acces to newer and more expansive communication technologies create new opportunities for human expression. But this major takes the tools as a means of expression -- not an end in themselves. The goal of this endeavor is to augment and improve human experience, and to bring both meaning and delight to people's lives.

If you're looking for a more detailed account, head over to our ABOUT page. If you'd rather take a look at our projects, we'd recommend our GALLERY.
We are dedicated to experiential learning --acquiring knowledge by making. Whether you like creative programming, cardboard cutouts, unexpected robotics or weaving LEDs in your clothes, we'd love to have you!

Our classes gravitate around three clusters -Stuff in JavaScript, Michael's Robots and a Bunch Of Readings; our ACADEMICS has a breakdown of our curriculum.
If you'd like to get a taste of Interactive Media, take a look at our ACTIVITIES page, where you can find a list of all our shows, workshops, and events.
