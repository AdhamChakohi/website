What does Interactive Media do?


Courses

We run courses ranging from Virtual Reality world-building to analog circuit-breaking. Our three tiered structure ensures that students get a strong foundation in physical computing, screen-based media, as well as critical thought and analysis of media theory.

Visit our courses page for more information on classes we offer.

Workshops

Our Sunday Workshops, which happen most weeks of each semester, are meant to be a relaxed, fun, and collaborative environment for learning and making. All our workshops are focussed on the practical; you'll usually leave having made something to take homne with you.

See the schedule of upcoming workshops.

Events

Interactive Media hosts several events each year, including end-of-semester showcases of student work, 24X, and the sort-of-annual GameJam.
